============================================
         AFTER TWEAK - READ THIS
============================================

1. Avoid GPU Driver Updates
   Updating your NVIDIA or AMD drivers resets custom settings and removes tweaks.
   Do NOT update your GPU driver without asking me first.

2. No External Tweaks
   Don't apply settings from YouTube, friends, or Discord.
   They can undo/worse your optimization.

3. Turn Off ALL Overlays
   Discord, Overwolf, and any other overlay must be turned off.
   Nothing should be running over your game.

4. Delete What You Don't Use
   Games, apps, files - if you don't use it, remove it.
   The less junk on your PC the better.

5. Run Clean.bat in your "After Tweak" Folder
   Open your After Tweak folder and run the Clean file.
   Do this regularly to keep your PC in shape.

6. Clean Out Your Downloads Folder
   Delete all setup and installer files in your Downloads folder.
   They are useless after the program is already installed.

7. Don't Modify the Setup
   Don't disable startup files or change system settings manually.
   The startup script applies key tweaks - leave it active.

8. Allow Shader Cache Time
   Games like Fortnite may have FPS drops during the first few matches.
   This is completely normal and temporary.

9. Having Issues? Contact Us
   Crashes, stutters, or performance loss?
   Reach out so we can fix it before it gets worse.
